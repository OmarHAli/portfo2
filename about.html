01 : About me

Background

I'm a business graduate that had initially gone down the traditional route of graduating college, getting a job, getting an MBA and everything else entailed in that all too familiar path. Deep down I've always had a passion for science and technology, and I have begun going down that path. I aim to develop these skills as I believe they will be very valuable in the future in both personally and professionally.

Coding Journey

I have spent the past few years dabbling with different programming languages until late last year when I decided to focus on Python, Data Science and Machine Learning.It's never easy learning something new, so I hope that by documenting my journey and sharing my ideas and experiences I can help others do the same as well as engage in discussions with peers in the field.

Data Science & Machine Learning

I believe that these two areas are very exciting given their importance to technology and business. As technology continues to advance and as we continue to generate more data, the demand to make use of that data and to improve technology accodingly increases. It is, in a way, a reinforcing loop.
